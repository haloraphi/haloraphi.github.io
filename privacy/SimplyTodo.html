Privacy Policy for Simply To Do

Thank you for using our to-do app. Your privacy is very important to us, so we would like to explain to you how we handle your data:

1. Data storage

All your created lists and tasks are stored exclusively on your device. We do not have access to this data and it is not stored on external servers.

2. Internet connection

Our app works completely offline. No internet connection is required to use the app. This means that no data is transmitted over the Internet.

3. Data collection

We do not collect any personal data or information about you or your use of the App. There are no analytics tools or similar mechanisms that monitor your activities.

4. Data access

The app only accesses the permissions necessary for its functionality, such as your device's storage to save your to-do lists locally.

This data protection declaration applies to the use of our to-do app. Please note that we may make updates to this statement to reflect changes in our privacy practices. It is recommended that you review this statement from time to time.
